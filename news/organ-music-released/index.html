March 12, 2019

Organ Music Released

Organist Susanne Kujala’s album Organ music of the 21st century featuring my piece Trois Mondes for organ and electronics was released by Alba Records.

The album presents pieces from five Finnish composers: Veli Kujala, Maija Hynninen, Minna Leinonen, Olli Virtaperko and Antti Auvinen. Organist Susanne Kujala, in turn, is known for actively performing, premiering and promoting contemporary music.

For more details, visit Organ music of the 21st century at the Alba Records website.
