September 1, 2021

Winner of the George Ladd Prix

I am happy to announce that I was selected as the winner of the George Ladd Prix de Paris by UC Berkeley in 2021.

The prize is awarded yearly to a graduate student at UC Berkeley Department of Music in recognition of the highest excellence in music composition. As the prize enables its recipient to study and compose music for one year in Paris, I will spend the time between 1st of October 2021 and 30th of April 2022 in France mainly working on my dissertation and a new chamber ensemble piece.
