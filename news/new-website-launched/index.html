May 4, 2023

New Website Launched

Spring is the time of transformation and change! And so, my website went through a transformation returning in its new form with an updated visual style in May 2023.

From now on, you can find the list of my works under distinct categories. You can also follow easily my upcoming and past performances in a new calendary-like layout. My biography text was as well renewed - go and have a read at About!

Here, in the News section, I will let you know about the most recent events in my career as well as write blog posts on music and composition from time to time. And as always, you can find the links to my music on SoundCloud and Spotify in the bottom of the page. As a new feature, from now on you can message me quickly through my contact form.

The design work for my new website as well as its development was done by composer and software engineer Heidi Hassinen.
