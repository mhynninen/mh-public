October 11, 2019

Dawn Breaks Released

My new album Dawn Breaks featuring selected works with electronics was released today by Ravello Records.

Dawn Breaks presents my pieces winnowing, ..sicut aurora procedit, Freedom from Fear and the Orlando-fragments, recorded between the years 2010-2019. Jaana Kärkkäinen (piano), Mirka Malmi (violin), Tuuli Lindeberg (soprano) and Kyle Bruckmann (oboe) are among the musicians performing my music on this record.

For more details, visit Dawn Breaks at the Ravello Records website.
