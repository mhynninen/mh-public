Highlights of 2022

In 2022 I…

  • was in residency at Cité International des Arts between January 1st and April 14th.

  • was in residency with Cras Ensemble in Denmark between January 20th and 23rd.

  • was in residency at IRCAM anechoic chamber between 7th and 11th of March, 17th and 18th of March as well as 4th and 14th of April.

  • gave a presentation lecture on Orchidea Orchestration tool and artistic work at IRCAM on the 25th of March.

  • graduated with a PhD from UC Berkeley!

  • started as a lecturer at the University of the Arts, Sibelius Academy, department of Music and Technology
